🥟 饺子小食谱 Simple Dumpling Recipe
材料 Ingredients:
• 饺子皮 dumpling wrappers
• 猪肉 pork / 蔬菜 vegetables
• 生姜 ginger · 酱油 soy sauce
步骤 Steps:
1. 拌馅 Mix the filling
2. 包饺子 Wrap the dumpling
3. 煮或蒸 Boil or steam
4. 蘸醋吃！Dip in vinegar & enjoy!
